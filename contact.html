Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can AIPay be integrated with our existing payment system?

Most AIPay solutions can be integrated within 4-6 weeks, depending on the complexity of your existing infrastructure. Our implementation team works closely with your technical staff to ensure a smooth integration process with minimal disruption to your operations.

Does AIPay offer solutions for specific payment verticals or industries?

Yes, we offer specialized solutions for various payment segments including payment processors, gateways, cross-border providers, mobile payment companies, and banking payment services. Our solutions can be tailored to address the unique challenges and requirements of your specific payment vertical.

How does AIPay ensure the security of payment data?

AIPay employs industry-leading security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure API communications, and regular security audits. Our solutions are PCI-DSS compliant and designed to help payment companies maintain compliance with relevant regulations such as GDPR, CCPA, and other regional data protection requirements.

What type of support does AIPay provide after implementation?

AIPay offers comprehensive ongoing support including 24/7 technical assistance, regular system updates, performance optimization consultations, and dedicated account management. Our client success team works proactively to ensure you're getting maximum value from our solutions.
