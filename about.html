About AIPay

Empowering payment companies with intelligent AI solutions

Our Mission

At AIPay, we're dedicated to transforming the payment industry through the power of artificial intelligence. Our mission is to help payment companies overcome the complex challenges they face in today's rapidly evolving digital economy.

We believe that payment providers are the backbone of global commerce, and by enhancing their capabilities, we help create a more efficient, secure, and accessible financial ecosystem for businesses and consumers worldwide.

Our Story

2018

Foundation

AIPay was founded by a team of payment industry veterans and AI specialists who recognized the potential for artificial intelligence to revolutionize payment processing, fraud prevention, and optimization.

2019

First Fraud Prevention System

We launched our first AI-powered fraud prevention system, helping payment processors reduce chargebacks by an average of 65% while decreasing false positives by 40%.

2020

Payment Optimization Suite

Introduced our comprehensive payment optimization suite, enabling payment gateways to increase authorization rates and reduce processing costs through intelligent routing algorithms.

2021

Business Intelligence Platform

Launched our advanced Business Intelligence platform, providing payment companies with actionable insights derived from their transaction data using proprietary AI models.

2022

Global Expansion

Expanded our operations to serve payment companies across North America, Europe, and Asia, with specialized solutions for regional payment challenges and regulatory environments.

2023

Integrated AI Platform

Released our unified AIPay Platform, integrating all our solutions into a cohesive ecosystem with advanced API connectivity and enhanced machine learning capabilities.
