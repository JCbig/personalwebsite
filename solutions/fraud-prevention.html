The Challenge

Payment companies face increasingly sophisticated fraud threats that evolve faster than traditional detection systems can adapt. False positives alienate legitimate customers, while missed fraud incidents lead to substantial financial losses and damaged reputation.

The AIPay Fraud Prevention Solution

Our advanced AI-powered fraud prevention system helps payment companies detect and prevent fraudulent transactions in real-time while reducing false positives.

Machine Learning Models

Our proprietary ensemble of machine learning models analyzes hundreds of data points per transaction to identify suspicious patterns invisible to rules-based systems.

Behavioral Biometrics

Advanced user behavior analytics detect anomalies in typing patterns, mouse movements, and session behaviors to identify fraudsters even when credentials are legitimate.

Network Intelligence

Our consortium database of fraud patterns across payment companies provides early warning of emerging threats before they impact your business.

Dynamic Risk Scoring

Contextual risk scoring adapts to your business model, customer base, and transaction patterns to optimize the balance between security and approval rates.

Real-Time Decisioning

Sub-100ms response times ensure your fraud prevention doesn't impact transaction speed or customer experience.

Continuous Improvement

Self-learning models continuously adapt to new fraud patterns and integrate feedback from manual reviews to improve detection accuracy.
